Pepperoni Pizza!

Pepperoni Pizza is a simple but delicious dish that anyone can prepare. I usually make this dish when my daughter and wife had a nice long day and we all feel like we can use a reward! Although Pizza is not the healthiest dish, it sure makes a good treat with your family. So lets get started!

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 25 mins

Servings: 8 servings

Ingredients

This is the list of ingredients you will need to make your delicious Pepperoni Pizza!:
  • 16 oz of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 oz mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shapping dough

Instructions

Now that we have all out ingredients ready, lets go ahead and start making a pizza pie!:
  1. Preheaat the oven to 500F.
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. If it is hard to roll, let it sit for 5 minutes to come to room temp.
  3. Add pizza sauce to pizza. Leave 1/4-inch crust around the edges.
  4. Lay half the pepperoni on the pizza.
  5. Top the pizza with the mozarella cheese, then with the rest of the pepperoni.
  6. Season the pizza with Black Pepper.
  7. Place piza onto a pizza stone or a heated skillet, then move into 500F oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Slice the pizza and enjoy!
